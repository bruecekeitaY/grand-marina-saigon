About Grand Marina Saigon

Grand Marina Saigon is the first Branded Residences project in Vietnam, carrying both Marriott and JW Marriott hotel brands. Developed by Masterise Homes, the project occupies the historic Ba Son site along the Saigon River in central District 1, Ho Chi Minh City.

With its irreplaceable location, exclusive Marriott brand, and pioneering Branded Residences model, Grand Marina Saigon is regarded as one of the most significant luxury real estate opportunities of the decade in HCMC.

3 reasons to choose Grand Marina Saigon

  • Exclusive Marriott brand — Vietnam's first Marriott Branded Residences; no equivalent project nearby.
  • Irreplaceable Ba Son location — the last large developable site in District 1 along the Saigon River.
  • Strong investment potential — Branded Residences typically command 25–35% price and rental premium over comparable apartments.
