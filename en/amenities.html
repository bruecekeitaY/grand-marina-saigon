Grand Marina Saigon Amenities

5-star Marriott hospitality amenities — live every day like you're on holiday.

  1. Home
  2. Amenities

The core difference between Branded Residences and standard apartments lies in the amenities and services. At Grand Marina Saigon, residents don't just own an apartment — they enjoy the complete 5-star living experience of Marriott International, right at home, 365 days a year.

Marriott-standard services

24/7 Concierge Service

Concierge staff trained by Marriott International, always ready to serve residents' needs:

  • Restaurant reservations locally and internationally
  • Flight and hotel bookings (Marriott Bonvoy benefits)
  • Luxury car & limousine airport transfers
  • Visa and documentation support for international guests
  • Personal assistant, package handling, personal shopping

Room Service & In-residence Dining

Order from JW Marriott / Marriott restaurants delivered to your door 24/7. The menu includes high-end Asian and European cuisine, signature drinks, and special dietary options.

Housekeeping & Laundry

5-star hotel-standard housekeeping and laundry service, available on schedule or on demand.

Valet Parking & 24/7 Security

Door-to-door valet parking, multi-layer security with CCTV, card/biometric access control, and professional security personnel.

Wellness & Recreation

Sky Infinity Pool

An infinity pool facing the Saigon River, on the upper floors of the tower — one of the most beautiful pools in HCMC. With separate children's pool, jacuzzi, and a poolside bar serving cocktails.

Fitness Center & Yoga Studio

Gym fully equipped with Technogym (Italy), yoga & pilates studio with private instructors, functional training room, boxing area.

Spa & Wellness

  • Therapeutic massage rooms (specialists from Thailand, Bali)
  • Dry sauna and steam rooms
  • Herbal bath room
  • Relaxation lounge

Social spaces

Sky Lounge & Library

Premium lounge on upper floors with panoramic Saigon views, serving afternoon tea and evening cocktails. Private library with 2,000+ international titles.

Boardroom & Private Dining Room

Residents can book private rooms for family gatherings or small meetings, with executive chef service from JW Marriott.

Cigar Lounge & Whisky Bar

An exclusive space for business leaders to unwind, with a curated collection of premium whisky and imported cigars.

Cinema Room

Private cinema with 4K screen, Dolby Atmos surround sound, premium reclining seats — complimentary booking for residents.

Family & Kids amenities

  • Kids Club: Indoor children's play area with professional supervision
  • Outdoor Playground: Safe outdoor play area with greenery
  • Mommy & Baby Lounge: Dedicated space for mothers and infants
  • Family Pool: Separate family pool with shallow area for children

Business amenities

  • Co-working Space: Shared work area with high-speed wifi
  • Meeting Rooms: Private meeting rooms with full conferencing equipment
  • Business Center: Printing, fax, scan, and secretarial support

Riverside green spaces

Grand Marina Saigon dedicates a significant portion of its area to landscape and green space:

  • Riverside Garden: Saigon riverside garden with sunset promenade
  • Central Lake: Central project lake with internationally designed landscape
  • Sky Garden: Sky garden at each tower
  • Yacht Club Access: Access to the Saigon River yacht harbor

Shopping & F&B inside the project

The podium level features a shopping arcade with:

  • International luxury boutiques
  • JW Marriott signature restaurants
  • Cafés & bakeries, deli shops
  • 24/7 premium mini-mart
  • Salon & barber shop

Marriott Bonvoy — Resident benefits

Grand Marina Saigon residents enjoy special Marriott Bonvoy privileges:

  • Discounts at 8,000+ Marriott hotels worldwide
  • Bonvoy points earning on in-project services
  • Marriott Vacation Club access
  • Room upgrades at Marriott properties globally
