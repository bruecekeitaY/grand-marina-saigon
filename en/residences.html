Grand Marina Saigon offers a wide variety of residence types — from compact 1-bedroom units suitable for investors and young couples, to expansive Penthouses for the discerning few. Every unit is designed and handed over to Marriott International standards, ensuring a globally recognized luxury living experience.

Project structure — 4 towers

Grand Marina Saigon consists of 4 residential towers, each carrying a different Marriott brand:

  • Lake Tower (Phase 2) — Marriott brand, 47 floors, 176.4m. Developer: Hưng Phát Investment. Interior by HBA.
  • Lagoon Tower (Phase 1) — JW Marriott brand, apartments & officetel.
  • Cove Tower (Phase 1) — JW Marriott brand, 47 floors, 184.2m. Developer: Elegance. Interior by AB Concept Hong Kong, architecture by ATKINS (UK).
  • Sea Tower (Phase 1) — Apartments under JW Marriott, Officetel under Marriott.

※ Phase 1 (Lagoon, Cove, Sea) handed over from December 2023. Phase 2 (Lake) handed over from March 2023.

1-Bedroom — Smart investment

Designed for space efficiency, ideal for rental investors targeting expat tenants or single buyers. With the Marriott brand, these units have rental potential of 25–40 million VND/month depending on view and floor.

  • Private bedroom with en-suite bathroom
  • Open living + kitchen layout
  • Balcony with river or interior view
  • Island kitchen with full imported appliances

2-Bedroom — Most popular choice

The most-selected type, balancing space and investment value. Suitable for young couples, small families, or premium rental investors.

  • 2 bedrooms with master en-suite
  • Spacious living area combined with kitchen
  • Separate dining area
  • Service balcony

3-Bedroom — Family space

Ideal for larger families or those with children. Comfortable common areas and private rooms for each member.

  • 3 separate bedrooms
  • Master with en-suite bathroom and walk-in closet
  • Separate living, dining and kitchen
  • Optional maid's room

Sky Villa & Penthouse — Pinnacle of luxury

Limited in number, Sky Villas and Penthouses at Grand Marina Saigon are among the rarest properties in Vietnam. Each Penthouse is bespoke-designed and includes:

  • Private plunge pool on the rooftop
  • Private garden
  • Private cinema / entertainment room
  • Dedicated private elevator
  • 270° view of Saigon River and city skyline

Handover standards

All units at Grand Marina Saigon are handed over fully finished to Marriott Branded Residences standards:

Interior finishes

  • Flooring: Imported marble or premium engineered wood
  • Kitchen: Poggenpohl/Boffi cabinetry, natural stone countertops, Miele/Gaggenau appliances
  • Bathroom: Toto/Duravit/Hansgrohe fittings, full marble cladding
  • Doors & hardware: Imported door systems, double-glazed soundproof windows
  • Air conditioning: Daikin VRV concealed-ceiling system
  • Smart home: Lighting, curtains, climate control via app

Services included

  • Marriott-standard 24/7 concierge
  • Room service from JW Marriott restaurants
  • Housekeeping
  • Valet parking
  • Restaurant, flight, and travel booking support

Limited availability

Each release phase introduces a limited number of units. Certain floors (high floors, panoramic views) sell out quickly. Please contact us early to secure premium units.
