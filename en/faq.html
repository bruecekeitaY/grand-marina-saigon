What is Grand Marina Saigon?
Grand Marina Saigon is the first Branded Residences project in Vietnam, carrying Marriott and JW Marriott brands. It is developed by Masterise Homes, located at Ba Son, District 1, Ho Chi Minh City, along the Saigon River.
How much does Grand Marina Saigon cost?
Reference pricing ranges from approximately $16,000–20,000 per m² for 1-bedroom units to $24,000+/m² for premium units. Total prices typically start from $800,000. Prices vary by tower, floor, view, and release phase. Please contact Zalo +84 903 475 802 for the latest exact pricing.
Can foreigners buy Grand Marina Saigon?
Yes. Foreigners can own Grand Marina Saigon units on a 50-year basis (renewable per regulations), with a maximum quota of 30% of total units per tower. The developer provides legal procedure support and bilingual documentation.
What are Branded Residences? How are they different?
Branded Residences are private apartments branded, operated, and managed by a luxury hotel group. Residents enjoy 5-star services like concierge, room service, and housekeeping at home. Compared to regular apartments, Branded Residences typically command 25–35% price premium and stronger rental and appreciation potential.
Has Grand Marina Saigon been handed over?
Yes, fully handed over. Lake Tower (Marriott, Phase 2) handed over from March 2023. The three JW Marriott towers (Lagoon, Cove, Sea — Phase 1) handed over from December 2023. Residents have moved in and Marriott concierge services are officially operational. You can move in immediately or participate in the secondary market (resale).
What payment options are available?
Three main options:
1. Standard progressive payment — approximately 25/75 or 30/70
2. Bank financing (Techcombank, VPBank, etc.) — principal & interest grace until handover
3. Fast payment 95–100% with 8–12% discount
What is the monthly management fee?
The official management fee is approximately $8–9 USD per m² per month (ex. VAT), including Marriott-standard services like concierge, system maintenance, 24/7 security, and common area housekeeping. The developer covers the management fee for the first 3 years — during this period residents only pay for electricity, water, and parking. After 3 years, Marriott invoices the management fee directly. There is also a one-time 2% maintenance fee at handover.
How long is the Marriott operating contract?
The operating contract between the developer and Marriott International is 20 years. After 20 years, the residents' management committee may negotiate renewal with Marriott or tender for a new operator. Individual owners cannot unilaterally terminate the contract with Marriott during the effective term.
Can I rent out my unit? What is the rental potential?
Yes, residents have full rights to rent out. With the Marriott brand and District 1 location, rental potential is strong:
  • 1 Bedroom: $1,000–1,600/month
  • 2 Bedroom: $1,600–2,800/month
  • 3 Bedroom: $2,800–5,000/month
Rental yield reaches 3.5–5% per year — high for the luxury segment.
How long is the ownership term?
Vietnamese buyers: Long-term (permanent) Pink Book — full rights to use, transfer, lease, and inherit.
Foreign buyers: 50-year ownership, renewable per Vietnamese law. Full rights to transfer and lease.
What Marriott Bonvoy benefits do residents get?
Grand Marina Saigon residents enjoy special Marriott Bonvoy privileges:
  • Discounts at 8,000+ Marriott hotels worldwide
  • Bonvoy points earning on in-project services
  • Room upgrades at other Marriott properties
  • Marriott Vacation Club access
What are the handover standards?
All units are handed over fully finished to Marriott Branded Residences standards: marble/engineered wood flooring, Poggenpohl/Boffi kitchen, Miele/Gaggenau appliances, Toto/Duravit bathroom fittings, Daikin VRV air conditioning, smart home system via app.
