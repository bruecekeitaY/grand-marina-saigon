Official project timeline

2024 — 2026

Residents move in & active secondary market

The project has completed handover of both Phase 1 and Phase 2. Residents have moved in and Marriott concierge services are officially operational. The secondary market (resale and rental) records strong liquidity.

12 / 2023

Phase 1 handover — Lagoon, Cove, Sea towers (JW Marriott)

The three JW Marriott Branded Residences towers were officially handed over on schedule. Total Phase 1 area: 16,866.5 m². Residents received fully finished units to JW Marriott standards.

03 / 2023

Phase 2 handover — Lake Tower (Marriott)

Lake Tower (Marriott brand) was the first tower to hand over: 47 floors, 176.4m tall, on 15,723.8 m² of land. Interior design by HBA, main contractor Newtecons.

2022

Lake Tower construction completion

Lake Tower completed its main structural work and entered the interior fit-out phase to Marriott Branded Residences standards.

2021

Sales Gallery launch

The Grand Marina Saigon showroom officially opened, featuring 1:1 show suites, the project model, and Marriott concierge service demonstrations.

2020 — 2021

Masterise Homes & Marriott International partnership announced

Masterise Homes officially announced its strategic partnership with Marriott International to develop Vietnam's first Marriott Branded Residences project. Marriott operating contract: 20 years.

Key news highlights

Price increases with each release phase

According to Masterise Homes, every Grand Marina Saigon release phase has recorded prices 5–15% above the previous release, reflecting strong demand and project scarcity. Early registrants typically secure the best prices.

Marriott International commits to operational management

Marriott International has confirmed it will directly operate services at Grand Marina Saigon — from concierge and room service to housekeeping management — ensuring a true 5-star global experience.

Metro Line 1 commercial operation

Metro Line 1 Ben Thanh — Suoi Tien has officially begun commercial operations, with Ba Son Station adjacent to Grand Marina Saigon. This is expected to create a major boost to real estate values in central District 1 along the Saigon River.

Branded Residences — 2026 investment trend

Knight Frank and Savills reports show Branded Residences as the fastest-growing segment in global luxury real estate. In Vietnam, Grand Marina Saigon leads this trend.

Early reservation promotions

In each phase, the developer typically offers special promotions for early registrants:

  • Discounts up to 5–10% of total value
  • Bonus furniture packages or 1-year management fee waiver
  • Priority selection of premium units
  • Marriott Bonvoy tier upgrades
